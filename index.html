About Me

Hi there! Welcome to my electronics portfolio. I'm a 16-year-old who's genuinely excited about learning how things work and building cool stuff with circuits and code. Every project you see here represents something I've tinkered with, broken, fixed, and learned from.

When I was around 10 I learnt about redstone in a game called Minecraft for the PlayStation 4, for those who don't, redstone in Minecraft enables the player to power certain blocks in the game such as: lamps, machines, doors ,and traps, which can be connected with redstone (similar to wire) and a button, ect. This is defiently where my intrest for electrical and hardware engineering begin.

My favorite parts about hardware and electronics is that you learn about a component by messing and playing around with it and its features. I've also enjoyed learning and working with microcontrollers, schematics and pcb design as well as soldering.

I also like car and bikes, my favorite ,at the moment, being the Koenigsegg Jesko for cars and the Suzuki Hayabusa for bikes. Apart from electrical engineering, Minecraft and cars, I love architecture, I have built many houses and structures in Minecraft to express myself through architecture. Some paint portraits, others sculpt sculptures, I build structures in Minecraft.

On that note, I hope You, the viewer, enjoy my little gadgets and gizmos and feel free to contact me. :D
